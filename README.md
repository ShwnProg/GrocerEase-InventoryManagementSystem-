# Grocery Inventory Management System

This project is a simple Inventory Management System for a grocery store, developed as part of our Information Management course. The system is designed to efficiently manage products, categories, and stock levels, ensuring accurate tracking of inventory.

# Features

## Dashboard

* Displays total products, categories, suppliers, and stocks
* Shows low stock and out-of-stock items

## Product Management

* Add, update, and delete products
* Assign products to categories and suppliers

## Category Management

* Add update delete categories

## Supplier Management

* Add, update, and manage supplier information

## Stock Management

* Monitor current stock levels
* Handle stock in and stock out transactions

## Inventory Logs

* Record and track all stock movements

## Archived Records

* View and manage deleted or archived data
